Each enemy can be categorized by their strength level. There are 4 Strength levels that you will encounter in the game, strength level 1, 2, 3, and 4. Enemies that have a strength level of 4 or higher are considered a boss enemy and usually have a fragment of the Elemental Diamond in their possesion or as their core. These Level 4 enemies also have the ability to command those below them in strength, only if they are of the same element.
